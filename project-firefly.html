Firefly

Firefly allows to send low bandwidth data like Wifi credentials and Account information to any digital device. This helps with the setup process of Smarthome devices lke Alexa and even to connect smartphones and laptops to a wifi network, without having to remeber it. It is able to use Bluetooth, Wifi and Our own proprietors technologies like Zeus' Bolt, Thor's Hammer, Merlins' Song. These use light emitter and light sensors on devices, such as ambient light sensor on phone and other devices.
